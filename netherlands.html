Netherlands

About

The Netherlands is a country where picturesque canals, colorful tulip fields, and charming villages create an unforgettable experience. With its rich history, vibrant cities, and stunning landscapes, the Netherlands offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and modern charm.

Highlights
Amsterdam
The capital city is famous for its iconic canals, world-class museums like the Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum, and the Anne Frank House. Explore its vibrant neighborhoods, enjoy a canal cruise, or rent a bike to experience the city like a local.
Kinderdijk
Visit this UNESCO World Heritage site, where 19 traditional windmills line the waterways, showcasing the Netherlands' historic battle with water management.
The Hague
As the political heart of the Netherlands, The Hague is home to the impressive Binnenhof, the Peace Palace, and beautiful seaside resorts like Scheveningen.
Zaanse Schans
Step back in time at this open-air museum, where you can explore traditional Dutch windmills, wooden houses, and craft workshops.
Keukenhof Gardens
Visit the world’s most famous flower garden in spring, where millions of tulips bloom in vibrant colors, creating a truly magical sight.
