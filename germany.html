Germany

About

Germany offers a perfect blend of rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes, making it a top destination for travelers. From fairy-tale castles and medieval towns to modern cities and picturesque countryside, Germany’s diverse offerings will captivate every type of traveler.

Highlights
Berlin
The capital city is a hub of history, art, and innovation. Visit the Berlin Wall, Brandenburg Gate, and the fascinating museums of Museum Island, or explore the vibrant neighborhoods filled with trendy cafes and galleries.
Neuschwanstein Castle
The fairy-tale castle that inspired Disney’s Sleeping Beauty Castle, perched high in the Bavarian Alps, is a must-see for any visitor.
Heidelberg
This charming university town along the Neckar River is known for its romantic castle and beautiful Old Town, perfect for a leisurely stroll.
Cologne Cathedral
A stunning example of Gothic architecture, the cathedral's towering spires dominate the skyline of this historic city on the Rhine River.
The Romantic Road
A scenic route winding through medieval villages, charming towns, and castles, offering a glimpse into Germany’s picturesque past.
