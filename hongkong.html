Hong Kong

About

These tours focus on exploring the urban side of Hong Kong, highlighting its famous landmarks, shopping districts, and cultural gems. Often guided, these tours provide a deep dive into the city’s rich history, architecture, and modern attractions.

Highlights
Hong Kong Disneyland Resort
A day at one of the world’s most famous theme parks, where you can enjoy attractions, shows, and parades based on Disney’s iconic characters and stories.
Hong Kong Island Tour
Discover the vibrant heart of the city, visiting popular spots such as Victoria Peak (for panoramic views), Man Mo Temple, Stanley Market, and Repulse Bay. Many tours include a tram ride or ferry cruise.
Kowloon & Tsim Sha Tsui Tour
Explore Kowloon’s bustling streets, with stops at the Hong Kong Museum of History, Temple Street Night Market, and the Star Ferry for a ride across Victoria Harbour. You may also visit the Avenue of Stars for views of the skyline.
Hong Kong Heritage Tour
Visit sites such as the Hong Kong Museum of History, the Wong Tai Sin Temple, the Kowloon Walled City Park, and the Tin Hau Temple, which reflect the city’s cultural evolution.
Sai Kung Peninsula & Beaches Tour
Explore the natural beauty of Sai Kung, with its rugged coastline, beaches, and clear waters. The area is perfect for hiking, swimming, and kayaking.
